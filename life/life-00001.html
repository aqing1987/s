Why Dota2 cannot be played from steam in China

Aug 8 2017

Table of Contents

  • 1. information
  • 2. solution

1 information

I'm into Dota2 from the very beginning. But last Month I cannot
play it in steam platform. The video is stucked, and My video
card is GTX660M.

BTW, I play Dota2 in China, use Win7 professional.

2 solution

I noticed that the steam Store cannot be displayed in the client. The steam
was blocked from GFW. Shit!

laod saved me!

Use laod's host to replace the Win7 default host file, I can play again!

You can get the host file from:

  • https://laod.cn/

Good luck!
